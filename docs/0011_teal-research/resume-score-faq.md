## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does the AI resume checker work?

The AI resume checker analyzes your content, [format](/resume-templates#format), and structure to provide a personalized resume score and actionable recommendations. It runs 15 targeted checks for ATS compatibility, keyword usage, and section-level performance—so you know exactly what to improve.

### How is the resume score calculated?

Your resume score is calculated based on key factors like structure, measurable results, keyword usage, and overall content strength. Each resume is scored on a 0–100% scale using best practices proven to influence hiring decisions.

### What is considered a good resume score?

A good resume score is 80% or higher. This means your resume meets most of the recommended standards and isn’t missing any critical information—so you’re more likely to stand out to both recruiters and applicant tracking systems.
