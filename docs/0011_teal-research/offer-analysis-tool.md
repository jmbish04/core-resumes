# Why should I use the Offer Analysis tool?

First off, congratulations on receiving a job offer! 🥳

A comprehensive offer letter usually contains details regarding your job terms, compensation (cash and variable), equity, and other employee benefits. When reviewing your offer, it's important to understand exactly what you are being offered before you make a decision about it.

Our Offer Analysis tool breaks down these details from your offer letter into easy-to-understand language. You'll also have the opportunity to ask our Compensation AI Agent as many follow-up questions as you'd like, until you've eliminated any ambiguities surrounding the offer and can make a more confident decision.

---

# Accessing the Offer Analysis

In order to access the Offer Analysis tool, you'll need to have **at least 5 bookmarked jobs** in your Job Tracker. These roles can be added [manually](https://help.tealhq.com/en/articles/9524931-bookmarking-a-job) or via our [Chrome extension](https://help.tealhq.com/en/articles/9524956-bookmarking-jobs-with-the-teal-chrome-extension).

Once you've bookmarked at least 5 jobs, you should see the "Offer Analysis" tab appear at the top of your Trackers page. Clicking into this tab will open your Offer Analysis tool.

## Analyze Additional Offers

After you've completed your first job offer analysis, you will not automatically be prompted to upload offer details upon opening the tool. Instead, you'll see a list of your previous offer analyses.

To add another offer from this page, click the "Create Offer" button and the upload menu will appear.

# Reviewing Your Offer Details

Once your job offer has been processed, you'll see it broken down into several key components: Key Terms, Cash Compensation, Variable Compensation, and Employment Terms.

## Key Terms

"Key Terms" lists your basic job details: Job Title, Work Start Date, Work Environment, and Employment Location.

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/x87je8q1/1304918964/583616c18b166a4ab698dacb27b2/Screenshot+2024-12-19+at+10_52_42%E2%80%AFAM.png?expires=1778347800&signature=4c77f684683f5b494a9fb5c83c344af01d5658abc92c5b1716bdfc68c9ac4214&req=dSMnEsB%2FlYhZXfMW1HO4zVQjQj%2BGtqrzf%2FntRhAHPwRFWtSz5LQkuELai%2F76%0ATXtg3norddR%2BYw7c%2F8s%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/x87je8q1/1304918964/583616c18b166a4ab698dacb27b2/Screenshot+2024-12-19+at+10_52_42%E2%80%AFAM.png?expires=1778347800&signature=4c77f684683f5b494a9fb5c83c344af01d5658abc92c5b1716bdfc68c9ac4214&req=dSMnEsB%2FlYhZXfMW1HO4zVQjQj%2BGtqrzf%2FntRhAHPwRFWtSz5LQkuELai%2F76%0ATXtg3norddR%2BYw7c%2F8s%3D%0A)

# Using the Compensation Agent

Once you've reviewed your offer components, you can ask our Compensation Agent any questions you may have about them!

Example questions can range from comparing your compensation against industry standards to benefits details. You can even ask the agent to provide examples of follow-up questions to ask the hiring manager directly!
